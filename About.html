My Background

The story began when I made the decision to start my own company with a friend. With our background in architecture and urban design, we focused on designing augmented reality games based on our knowledge of urban design. We were passionate about our careers and devoted ourselves to its growth.

Consequently, I decided to learn website development, which marked the start of an exciting adventure. I taught myself how to design and implement a website, which led me to create our company's website. We achieved numerous accomplishments and received awards, establishing ourselves as a thriving business. However, the advent of the COVID era, unfortunately led to the closure of our company.

Despite this setback, I continued my journey in web design, particularly focusing on WordPress. During that period, I realized that I enjoyed web design more than architecture. Through self-study, I explored different design areas, including graphic design, logo design, poster design, and motion graphics. Exploring these fields helped me finally realized that UI/UX design is where my interests lie.
